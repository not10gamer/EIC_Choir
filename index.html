ABOUT THE CHOIR

The EIC Senior Choir is a vibrant community of passionate singers dedicated to musical excellence and artistic expression. Founded 2 years ago, we have grown into one of the most respected choral groups in the region, known for our dynamic performances and diverse repertoire.

Our mission is to foster a lifelong love of music, build a strong sense of community, and create unforgettable experiences for both our members and our audiences. We believe in the power of music to unite, inspire, and transform.
